Cheesecake Recipe

How to make the perfect plain cheescake to appreciate the beauty of simplicity.

This regular plain cheescake may not be the most extravagent looking dessert but sometimes you need to learn to appreciate the simple things in life.

WHoever loves cheescake is automatically my friend thats for sure, I just hope there will be other people to share this wonderful dessert with as well once you make THE BEST CHEESCAKE IN THE WORLD!!!

Ingredients

  • Bread Crumbs
  • Creamcheese
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the creamcheese with the eggs and sugar and salt until thick
  2. Add the sugar and salt to your prefered taste - usually more sugar than salt
  3. Use some creamcheese to hold the bread crumbs into a cake shape
  4. Place mixture into cake or simply on top of the bread crumbs
  5. Put in fridge to cool and stiffen
  6. Take out and enjoy this amazing desser
