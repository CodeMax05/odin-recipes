Lasagna Recipe

How to make a beautiful lasagna that even the angry neighbor next door will have to love!!!

This recipe is simple yet complex, basic yet extravagent and mosty of all DELICIOUS and will only take a few hours of your day to prepare for the whole family

You must prepare the pasta so that its soft enough yet firm enough that when you finish making the lasagna it still has room to cook without falling apart. Also you must prepare the beef with only the necessary spices so its not overpowering. Lastly, the tomato sauce is best when made fresh from tomatoes and only needs a few extra ingredients to make it wonderful

Sometimes less is more :)

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Love

Steps

  1. Boil salted water
  2. Cook pasta sheets for 10-20 mins
  3. Cook ground beef on stainless steel pan for flavor
  4. take out pasta sheets and place in heat resistant container
  5. Layer the ingredients like a sandwich - Pasta, Beef, Mozarella, Cottage Cheese, Pasta
  6. Cook in oven for 20 minutes
  7. Take out and enjoy a beutiful Lasagna
