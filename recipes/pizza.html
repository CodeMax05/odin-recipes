How to make the perfect Pepperoni Pizza - It's very Delicious

While we all know that this pizza is very very simple it takes a certain gental hand to make it amazing

It is so simple even I could do it, its just dough, tomato sauce, cheese and pepperoni. So simple that if you can't figure it out then there might be something wrong with you

Ingredients

  • Dough - flour, water and yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzeralla cheese - preferably shredded
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. spread and stretch the dough out into a nice medium sized circle or as big as you want
  3. place a decent amount of tomato sauce all over the douch leaving the edgess for the crust
  4. next put the cheese evenly over the pizza
  5. then do the same with the pepperoni
  6. place pizza in over at high heat for 5-10 minutes
  7. take out and enjoy the delicious pizza you made
